<p><p>The household coffee maker market can be categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic machines. Manual coffee makers require users to control each brewing step, offering a hands-on experience. Semi-automatic models automate some processes, like water temperature, while still allowing user involvement in grinding and tamping. Fully automatic coffee makers handle the entire brewing process, from grinding to brewing, at the touch of a button, providing convenience and consistency for users seeking a quick and easy coffee experience.</p></p>
