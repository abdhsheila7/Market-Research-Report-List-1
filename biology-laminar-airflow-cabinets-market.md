<p><p>Biology laminar airflow cabinets are essential equipment used in laboratories to provide a sterile environment by preventing contamination during experiments. The market is categorized into two primary types: horizontal and vertical laminar airflow cabinets. Horizontal cabinets direct airflow from the back towards the front, ensuring unobstructed lines of sight and ease of access for users. In contrast, vertical cabinets draw air from above, providing better protection for sensitive samples and maintaining uniform airflow across the work surface. Both types are crucial for various biological applications.</p></p>
