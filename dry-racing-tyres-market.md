<p><strong>The Dry Racing Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slick Tyres</li><li>Semi Slick Tyres</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry racing tyres market primarily comprises slick tyres and semi-slick tyres. Slick tyres feature a completely smooth surface, optimizing grip on dry tracks, enhancing performance and speed during races. They are ideal for dry conditions, providing maximum contact with the ground. In contrast, semi-slick tyres have a tread pattern that offers some water dispersion capabilities while still maintaining a majority slick surface. This makes them versatile for slightly damp or mixed conditions, striking a balance between traction and performance.</p></p>
