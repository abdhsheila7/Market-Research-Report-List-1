<p><p>The Nano Titanium Dioxide market is primarily categorized into three types: Anatase, Rutile, and Other. Anatase type features higher photocatalytic activity, making it suitable for applications in sun-blocking agents and self-cleaning surfaces. Rutile type, known for its superior UV stability and durability, is predominantly used in paints, coatings, and plastics. The "Other" category encompasses specialized formulations and blends tailored for niche applications, including electronics and cosmetics, leveraging unique properties of titanium dioxide to meet specific performance needs.</p></p>
