<p><ul><li>BTAC</li><li>BTMS</li><li>STMS</li><li>CTMS</li><li>SAPDA</li><li>BAPDA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cationic surfactant market encompasses various types, each with unique applications. BTAC (Benzyl Trimethyl Ammonium Chloride) is commonly used in cosmetics and personal care products. BTMS (Behentrimonium Methosulfate) serves as a conditioning agent in hair care. STMS (Stearyl Trimethyl Ammonium Chloride) is popular in fabric softeners. CTMS (Cetyl Trimethyl Ammonium Chloride) finds use in hair and skin products. SAPDA (Sodium Alkyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride) and BAPDA (Benzyl Alkyl Dimethyl Ammonium Chloride) are effective antimicrobial agents, widely utilized in disinfectants and preservatives.</p></p>
