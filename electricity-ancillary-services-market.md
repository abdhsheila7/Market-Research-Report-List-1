<p><p>Electricity ancillary services are essential for maintaining the reliability and stability of power systems. Frequency Control Service ensures that the grid frequency remains stable by adjusting generation or load in real time. Spinning Reserve Service involves on-demand backup power from online resources that can quickly respond to sudden supply shortages. Operating Reserve Service is the total available capacity that can be activated to meet unexpected demand spikes or generation failures, ensuring a reliable supply of electricity.</p></p>
