<p><strong>The NB IoT Chipset Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standalone</li><li>In-Band</li><li>Guard Band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NB IoT chipset market is categorized into three types: Standalone, In-Band, and Guard Band. Standalone operates on dedicated spectrum, enhancing efficiency for IoT devices in remote areas. In-Band utilizes existing LTE channels, optimizing resources without disrupting current services, making it suitable for urban deployments. Guard Band, on the other hand, leverages unused frequencies between LTE channels, ensuring minimal interference while expanding IoT connectivity options. Each type addresses specific deployment needs and regulatory requirements within the IoT ecosystem.</p></p>
