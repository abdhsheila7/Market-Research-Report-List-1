<p><ul><li>Electronically Controlled Air Suspension</li><li>Non-Electronically Controlled Air Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Passenger Car Air Suspension Market is divided into two main types: Electronically Controlled Air Suspension (ECAS) and Non-Electronically Controlled Air Suspension. ECAS utilizes sensors and electronic systems to adjust air pressure in the air springs, optimizing ride quality and stability based on real-time conditions. In contrast, Non-Electronically Controlled Air Suspension relies on a more traditional mechanical approach, providing a basic level of air spring adjustment without advanced electronic features, generally offering a simpler and less expensive solution.</p></p>
