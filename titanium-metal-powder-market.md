<p><p>The Titanium Metal Powder market is primarily categorized into two types: High Purity Titanium Metal Powder (HPTP) and Alloyed Titanium Metal Powder (ATP). HPTP is characterized by its superior purity, making it ideal for critical applications in aerospace, medical implants, and high-performance components. In contrast, ATP comprises titanium blended with various alloying elements, enhancing mechanical properties and corrosion resistance, which is essential for automotive and industrial applications. Both types serve diverse sectors, driving demand through their unique qualities.</p></p>
