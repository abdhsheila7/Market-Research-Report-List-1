<p><strong>The PVC Blowing Agent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exothermic Blowing Agent</li><li>Endothermic Blowing Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVC blowing agent market is categorized into two main types: exothermic and endothermic blowing agents. Exothermic blowing agents generate heat during the foaming process, enhancing cell structure and improving end-product density, often used in rigid PVC applications. In contrast, endothermic blowing agents absorb heat, leading to a cooling effect that aids in achieving a lighter, more flexible foam structure, suitable for applications requiring better insulation and elasticity. Both types cater to different PVC product requirements in various industries.</p></p>
