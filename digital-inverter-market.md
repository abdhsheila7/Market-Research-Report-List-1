<p><p>The digital inverter market is categorized into three main types: central inverters, string inverters, and micro inverters. Central inverters are large units typically used in utility-scale solar plants, managing multiple solar panels efficiently. String inverters connect several panels in a series, optimizing performance for residential and commercial systems. Micro inverters are smaller devices installed on individual panels, allowing for maximum energy production and enabling panel-level monitoring. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to different operational needs and installation scales.</p></p>
