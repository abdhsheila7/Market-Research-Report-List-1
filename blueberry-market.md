<p><ul><li>Highbush</li><li>Lowbush</li><li>Hybrid half-high</li><li>Rabbiteye</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blueberry market types include Highbush, Lowbush, Hybrid half-high, and Rabbiteye. Highbush blueberries thrive in well-drained soils, yielding large, tart berries suitable for fresh consumption. Lowbush blueberries, often wild, produce smaller, sweeter fruits and are typically used for processing. Hybrid half-high varieties combine traits of both, being cold-hardy and adaptable for various climates. Rabbiteye blueberries, popular in the southern U.S., are heat-tolerant and offer a diverse range of flavors, catering to different consumer preferences and growing conditions.</p></p>
