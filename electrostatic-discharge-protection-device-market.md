<p><ul><li>Self Restoring Fuse</li><li>Thermal Fuse</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrostatic discharge (ESD) protection device market includes several key types: self-restoring fuses, thermal fuses, and other protective components. Self-restoring fuses automatically reset after an ESD event, making them ideal for preventing device downtime. Thermal fuses provide protection by melting and interrupting the circuit in response to excessive heat generated during a discharge. Other market types encompass diverse devices like transient voltage suppressors and varistors, which offer additional layers of protection against voltage spikes and surges in electronic systems.</p></p>
