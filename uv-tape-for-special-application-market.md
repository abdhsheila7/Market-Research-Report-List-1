<p><p>UV tape for special applications is designed for precise bonding and masking in environments exposed to ultraviolet light. The market is divided into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided UV tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for securing components or protecting surfaces during UV exposure. In contrast, double-sided UV tape has adhesive on both sides, providing stronger adhesion and versatility for applications requiring bonding two surfaces together. Both types are critical in industries like electronics, automotive, and medical device manufacturing.</p></p>
