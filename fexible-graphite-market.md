<p><ul><li>Isotropic Graphite</li><li>Extruded Graphite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible graphite market includes two main types: isotropic graphite and extruded graphite. Isotropic graphite is characterized by uniform properties in all directions, making it ideal for applications requiring consistent performance, such as thermal management and sealing solutions. Extruded graphite, on the other hand, is produced through a process that aligns the graphite flakes, resulting in enhanced performance in specific directions, making it suitable for applications including electrodes and thermal pads. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
