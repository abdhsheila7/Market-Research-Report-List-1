<p><p>The premix insulin market consists of two primary types: premixed human insulin and premixed insulin analogues. Premixed human insulin combines rapid-acting and intermediate-acting insulins to provide control over blood sugar levels after meals and throughout the day. In contrast, premixed insulin analogues are genetically modified forms of insulin that offer improved absorption and peak action times, enhancing glycemic control. Both types cater to individuals with diabetes, providing tailored options to manage their condition effectively.</p></p>
