<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Chemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rapeseed oilseed processing market is primarily divided into mechanical and chemical processing methods. Mechanical processing involves physical extraction techniques, such as expeller pressing, which utilize mechanical force to extract oil from seeds without the use of solvents. This method is often seen as more natural and eco-friendly. In contrast, chemical processing employs solvents, typically hexane, to achieve higher oil yields from the seeds. This method is more efficient in extraction but may involve further refining to remove residual solvents, affecting purity.</p></p>
