<p><strong>The Solid Phase Extraction SPE Disk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversed-Phase Solid phase extraction (SPE)</li><li>Ion-Exchange Solid phase extraction (SPE)</li><li>Normal Phase Solid phase extraction (SPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) disk market comprises three primary types: </p><p>1. **Reversed-Phase SPE** focuses on separating non-polar compounds using hydrophobic interactions with a stationary phase, widely used for pharmaceutical and environmental samples.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Ion-Exchange SPE** utilizes charged stationary phases to attract ions from solutions, suitable for separating charged molecules like proteins.</p><p>3. **Normal Phase SPE** employs polar stationary phases to separate polar compounds, often used in organic synthesis and purification. Each type is tailored for specific applications in analytical chemistry.</p></p>
