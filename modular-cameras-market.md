<p><strong>The Modular Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular cameras market primarily consists of two sensor types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and low noise levels, making them suitable for professional applications. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and larger integration capabilities, which appeal to consumer devices and real-time imaging applications. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including security, healthcare, automotive, and consumer electronics, driving market growth.</p></p>
