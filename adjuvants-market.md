<p><p>The adjuvants market is diverse, comprising various types that enhance vaccine effectiveness. Adjuvant emulsions facilitate immune responses through oil-in-water or water-in-oil mixtures. Pathogen components, derived from microbial structures, stimulate innate immunity. Particulate adjuvants, such as polymers or nanoparticles, promote antigen presentation and immune system activation. Combination adjuvants leverage multiple mechanisms, integrating different strategies to optimize vaccine efficacy. Collectively, these adjuvant types play crucial roles in the development of effective vaccines and immunotherapies.</p></p>
