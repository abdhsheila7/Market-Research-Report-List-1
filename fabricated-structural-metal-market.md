<p><p>The fabricated structural metal market is divided into two main types: light sectional steel and heavy sectional steel. Light sectional steel typically includes components like beams, channels, and angles that are thinner and used in applications such as residential buildings and smaller structures. In contrast, heavy sectional steel comprises thicker, more robust elements designed for larger, more demanding projects, including industrial buildings and bridges. Both types are essential for providing structural integrity in various construction applications.</p></p>
