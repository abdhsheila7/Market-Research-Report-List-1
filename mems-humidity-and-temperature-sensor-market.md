<p><p>The MEMS humidity and temperature sensor market consists of two primary types: digital and analog sensors. Digital sensors provide output in a digital format, allowing for easy integration with microcontrollers and digital systems, ensuring higher accuracy and precision. In contrast, analog sensors generate continuous signals proportional to humidity and temperature changes, often requiring additional circuitry for interpretation. Both types serve diverse applications, including HVAC systems, consumer electronics, and environmental monitoring, catering to varying needs for data processing and system design.</p></p>
