<p><p>The MMA (Methyl Methacrylate) monomer market features distinct production methods: the ACH method, isobutylene method, and ethylene method. The ACH method utilizes acetone cyanohydrin as a precursor, ensuring high purity levels. The isobutylene method combines isobutylene with methacrylic acid, providing an efficient process with favorable yields. The ethylene method involves the reaction of ethylene and methacrylic acid, promoting cost-effectiveness. Each method caters to specific industry requirements, influencing the overall market dynamics and application across various sectors like automotive and construction.</p></p>
