<p><strong>The Fixed X-Ray Flat Panel Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Conversion</li><li>Indirect Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fixed X-Ray Flat Panel Detector market is categorized into two main types: direct conversion and indirect conversion. Direct conversion detectors use materials that convert x-rays directly into electrical signals, resulting in high image quality and enhanced sensitivity. In contrast, indirect conversion detectors first convert x-rays into light, which is then transformed into an electrical signal. While indirect systems are generally less expensive and easier to manufacture, direct systems are favored for their superior image resolution and lower radiation exposure.</p></p>
