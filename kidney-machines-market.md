<p><p>The kidney machines market encompasses various treatments for renal failure. Continuous Blood Purification (CBP) involves a constant removal of toxins from the blood, beneficial for critically ill patients. Hemodialysis (HD) is a more common method, utilizing external machines to filter blood and remove waste, typically performed several times a week. Other market segments may include peritoneal dialysis and supportive devices that assist kidney function, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in renal care.</p></p>
