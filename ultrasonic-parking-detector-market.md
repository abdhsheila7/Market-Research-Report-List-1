<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Parking Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Doppler Parking Detector"</li><li>"Sound Field Parking Detector"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Parking Detector market comprises two main types: Doppler Parking Detectors and Sound Field Parking Detectors. Doppler Parking Detectors utilize the Doppler effect to measure changes in frequency caused by moving objects, allowing for real-time distance calculation. In contrast, Sound Field Parking Detectors operate by emitting ultrasonic waves that create a sound field, detecting obstacles based on reflected sound waves. Both technologies enhance parking safety by alerting drivers to nearby objects, thereby preventing collisions and ensuring a smoother parking experience.</p></p>
