<p><p>The hygienic hand dryer market is primarily segmented into two types: jet hygienic hand dryers and hot hygienic hand dryers. Jet hygienic hand dryers utilize high-velocity air streams to rapidly dry hands, often reducing drying time and minimizing water splatter. In contrast, hot hygienic hand dryers blow warm air to evaporate moisture, providing a gentle drying experience. Both types prioritize hygiene by eliminating the need for paper towels, promoting cleanliness, and reducing environmental waste in public and commercial spaces.</p></p>
