<p><p>Helical gearmotors are vital in various industries, available primarily in two market types: hollow shaft and solid shaft. Hollow shaft gearmotors feature a central opening, allowing for easy connection to shafts or other equipment, enhancing flexibility in installation. Solid shaft gearmotors, on the other hand, have a solid output shaft that provides increased durability and strength, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types serve distinct operational needs, influencing their applications across manufacturing, automotive, and conveyor systems.</p></p>
