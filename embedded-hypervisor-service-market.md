<p><strong>The Embedded Hypervisor Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bare-Metal Hypervisors</li><li>Hosted Hypervisors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Embedded Hypervisor Service market is divided into two primary types: bare-metal hypervisors and hosted hypervisors. Bare-metal hypervisors operate directly on the host's hardware, providing enhanced performance and efficiency for virtual machines by minimizing overhead. They are ideal for environments requiring high resource allocation. In contrast, hosted hypervisors run on top of an existing operating system, simplifying installation and usability but potentially introducing more latency. Both types cater to diverse virtualization needs, balancing performance and ease of use.</p></p>
