<p><p>The powered surgical instruments market encompasses various types based on their power source. Battery-powered devices offer portability and flexibility, often used in remote or minimally invasive procedures where mobility is crucial. Pneumatic-powered instruments utilize compressed air, delivering high torque and efficiency for specific surgical applications. Each type is designed to enhance precision, reduce manual effort, and improve surgical outcomes across diverse medical fields.</p></p>
