<p><p>The Medium Voltage Plastic Film Capacitors market primarily comprises two types: Polyester Film Capacitors and Metallized Film Capacitors. Polyester Film Capacitors are known for their durability and cost-effectiveness, often used in power electronics for energy storage and filtering applications. Metallized Film Capacitors, on the other hand, feature a thin metal layer which enhances performance by reducing losses and improving self-healing properties. Both types play crucial roles in power systems, providing stability and enhancing the efficiency of electrical circuits.</p></p>
