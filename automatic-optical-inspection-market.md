<p><strong>The Automatic Optical Inspection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Optical Inspection (AOI) market is divided into two primary types: inline AOI equipment and offline AOI equipment. Inline AOI systems are integrated directly into the production line, allowing real-time inspection during the manufacturing process, which enhances efficiency and minimizes defects. In contrast, offline AOI equipment operates separately from the production line, enabling detailed inspections after production runs, which can be beneficial for more comprehensive quality control and failure analysis. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product quality and reliability.</p></p>
