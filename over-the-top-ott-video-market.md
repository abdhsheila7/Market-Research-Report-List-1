<p><p>The Over-The-Top (OTT) video market consists of various delivery models. Subscription Video on Demand (SVOD) allows users to pay a recurring fee for unlimited content access. Transactional Video on Demand (TVOD) enables users to purchase or rent content on a pay-per-view basis. Advertising-based Video on Demand (AVOD) offers free content supported by ads, generating revenue through advertising. Other market types may include hybrid models or niche services that combine elements of these primary categories to cater to specific audience preferences.</p></p>
