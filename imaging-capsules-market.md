<p><p>The imaging capsules market consists of three main types: esophageal capsules, small bowel capsules, and colon capsules. Esophageal capsules are designed for visualizing the esophagus to diagnose conditions like dysphagia. Small bowel capsules facilitate the examination of the small intestine, helping to detect issues such as tumors or Crohn's disease. Colon capsules are aimed at visualizing the colon for evaluating colorectal diseases, providing an alternative to traditional colonoscopy. Each type serves a specific diagnostic purpose, enhancing minimally invasive imaging approaches.</p></p>
