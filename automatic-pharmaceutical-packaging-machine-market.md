<p><p>The automatic pharmaceutical packaging machine market comprises three key types: primary packaging machines, secondary packaging machines, and labeling and serialization machines. Primary packaging machines focus on enclosing the product directly, ensuring product integrity and safety. Secondary packaging machines group primary packages for bulk handling and distribution, often enhancing shelf appeal. Labeling and serialization machines apply essential product information and unique identifiers for traceability, compliance, and anti-counterfeiting measures, playing a crucial role in efficient supply chain management within the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
