<p><p>The laboratory faucets market is categorized into three main types: single-hole, two-hole, and three-hole faucets. Single-hole faucets feature a single mounting point, offering a streamlined design suitable for limited space. Two-hole faucets provide additional versatility with separate hot and cold water controls but require more installation space. Three-hole faucets incorporate a spout with two handles, allowing precise temperature control. Each type is designed to meet varied laboratory needs, emphasizing functionality, maintenance, and ease of use in laboratory environments.</p></p>
