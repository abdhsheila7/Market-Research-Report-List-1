<p><p>Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) are essential for rendering images and graphics in computing devices. The market is divided into two main types: Independent GPUs and Integrated GPUs. Independent GPUs, often used in gaming and professional applications, are dedicated hardware with high performance and power, catering to users requiring advanced graphics capabilities. In contrast, Integrated GPUs are built into the same chip as the CPU, offering a cost-effective solution for everyday tasks, suitable for casual gaming and basic graphic needs.</p></p>
