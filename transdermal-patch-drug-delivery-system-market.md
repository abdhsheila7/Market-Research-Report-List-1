<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Transdermal Patch Drug Delivery System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Way</li><li>Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway</li><li>Trans-cellular Pathway</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transdermal patch drug delivery system encompasses three primary market types based on the pathway through which drugs penetrate the skin. </p><p>1. **Shunt Way**: Involves drug delivery through hair follicles and sweat glands, allowing substances to bypass the stratum corneum. </p><p>2. **Intercellular Lipid Structure Pathway**: Relies on the natural lipid matrix between skin cells, facilitating drug diffusion through the intercellular spaces. </p><p>3. **Trans-cellular Pathway**: Requires drugs to penetrate directly through skin cells, allowing for targeted and efficient delivery. Each pathway offers distinct advantages for drug absorption and therapeutic efficacy.</p></p>
