<p><p>Drill bits in the oil and gas market are crucial tools used for drilling through geological formations. There are two primary types: Fixed Cutter Drill Bits and Roller Cone Drill Bits. Fixed cutter bits use hard materials like diamonds or carbide for cutting, ideal for hard rock formations. In contrast, roller cone bits have rotating cones equipped with sharp teeth that crush rock. Each type serves specific drilling conditions, influencing efficiency, cost, and wellbore stability in exploration and production activities.</p></p>
