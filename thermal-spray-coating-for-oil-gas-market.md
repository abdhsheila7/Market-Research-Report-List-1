<p><p>Thermal spray coating in the oil and gas market involves various techniques to enhance the durability and performance of components. Cold spray utilizes kinetic energy for coating, minimizing thermal impact. Flame spray melts particles using an oxy-fuel flame. Plasma spray involves ionizing gas to create high-temperature molten particles for coating applications. High-velocity oxygen fuel (HVOF) offers high-density coatings through accelerated combustion. Electric arc spray uses an electric arc to melt feedstock. Other methods include deposition techniques tailored for specific operational environments.</p></p>
